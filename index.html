RS3 F2P IRONMAN ESSENTIALS

A safe Hardcore Ironman progression route

Quest before grinding

Complete early RuneScape 3 F2P quests such as The Restless Ghost, The Knight’s Sword and Witch’s House before long training sessions. Their rewards skip slow early levels and unlock safer equipment, food and travel routes.

Build HCIM safety first

Prioritise reliable food, tier-appropriate armour, emergency teleports and level 43 Prayer before dangerous bosses. Wilderness bosses and dragon routes remain optional because preserving Hardcore Ironman status matters more than maximum XP per hour.

Connect every level 99

Mine ores for Smithing, cut logs for Fletching and Firemaking, and cook the fish gathered for Fishing. This self-sufficient F2P Ironman approach reduces duplicated work while building combat supplies for Ivar, Giant Mole and King Black Dragon.
